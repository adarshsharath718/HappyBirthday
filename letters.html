The Proposal

Yours,

The Journey

Hey Love,

From casual snaps to deep conversations, it all began with just a few filters and playful messages. Who knew that behind every silly face and emoji, something real was growing? I found myself waiting for your snap, smiling at your replies, and slowly, unknowingly, falling for you.

As the days went by, our conversations deepened. We moved past filters and selfies into late-night chats and morning greetings. Those moments became the soft threads that stitched us closer. You became my comfort space—someone I could laugh with, open up to, and be entirely myself around.

And then we met—truly met. That first time, my heart was racing. Your shy smile, the way you looked at me, and the silence we both didn’t mind—it all felt surreal. It wasn’t just a meeting. It was magic unfolding in front of my eyes.

Then came that unforgettable night—Ganesh Chaturthi. I don’t know what took over me, but suddenly, I found myself telling you what I’d been feeling for so long. And you said “yes.” That moment shifted something inside me. Everything felt lighter, warmer, right.

But what truly moved me was what came next: “Will you convince my parents?” That question hit deep. It wasn’t just about us anymore—it was about everything we wanted to build. I knew then that this love was real, serious, and worth every step.

Our journey since has been filled with the sweetest memories—meeting on weekends, eating our favorite food, sharing laughs, and sitting quietly when words weren’t needed. Every hug, every goodbye, every plan for the next time kept me going.

Of course, not every day was perfect. We faced our share of misunderstandings, distance, and tough moments. But through it all, we chose each other. Again and again. And that’s what love is, isn’t it? Not perfect, but persistent.

We’ve grown together—learning, unlearning, supporting, and loving. You’ve been my biggest strength and my safest place. And with every moment, I feel more certain about us, our journey, and the life we’re slowly building.

I know there’s still a long road ahead—many more steps, many more moments to cherish, and yes, many more challenges to face. But I’m ready. With you by my side, I’m ready for it all.

Forever yours,
PAPACHI ❤️

The Future

Hey Love,

As I sit and think about everything we’ve been through, my heart fills with gratitude—not just for our past, but for everything we’re yet to build. We've come a long way, and yet the most beautiful part is knowing how much more lies ahead of us.

I promise to stand by you through every academic milestone. Whether it's preparing for exams, cheering you on through interviews, or simply helping you stay calm during the stress of it all—I will be your biggest supporter. Your growth inspires me, and together, I know we’ll keep pushing each other to become the best versions of ourselves.

Spiritually too, I promise to grow with you. To find peace in the little things, to pray not just for myself, but for our future, and to always believe that there's a higher power watching over our journey. I will sit with you in silence when you need strength, and I will whisper hope into your heart when the world feels heavy.

Most of all, I promise to nurture our togetherness. To protect the bond we’ve built, to be present in both the ordinary and the extraordinary moments. Whether it's sitting in a cafe after a long week, taking a walk under the stars, or simply doing nothing together—I’ll treasure it all.

I know life won't always be smooth. There will be days when we feel tired, unsure, or even distant. But in those moments, I promise to remember why we began. I promise to communicate with kindness, to forgive with an open heart, and to hold onto the love we chose every single day.

With every success we achieve—individually or together—I want us to celebrate not just the destination, but the journey we took to get there. I want to build a future with you that is not just filled with achievements, but with meaning, balance, and peace.

So here’s my vow: to grow with you, dream with you, cry and laugh with you, and walk beside you in every season—until forever doesn't feel long enough.

With all my heart,
PAPACHI ❤️
